Fruit Tier List

A comprehensive ranking of every fruit*, given by a boy whose opinions do not matter

As defined by a semi-comprehensive Wikipedia list on the subject

But first, some ground rules:

  • We're talking culinary fruits. Tomatoes are off the list, just accept it.
  • This is a list of my opinions. Mangos are gross, okay?
  • Different varities of the same fruit aren't differently ranked. I'm not a chef, sheesh.
  • If I've not tried it, it's unranked. Journalistic integrity.
  • If I've not heard of it, it is off the list. Sue me.

Doing some clean-up work, this leaves approximately 75 fruits on the cutting board.
